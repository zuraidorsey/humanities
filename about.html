Biography

I am Zurai Dorsey, a fourth-year student at the University of Texas at Austin pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in an Honors Pre-Law Individualized Major focused on International Political Economy with a concentration in AI and Cybersecurity. I am also pursuing a second Bachelor of Arts in Government, with minors in Business and History.

As a first-generation college student, I navigate academic and professional spaces without inherited institutional networks. This experience has shaped my leadership, resilience, and commitment to expanding access to legal and regulatory education. I approach governance and policy work with both structural awareness and intellectual ambition.

My work focuses on international AI governance, cybersecurity regulation, and intellectual property law—particularly their impact on multinational technology and creative industries. I am deeply interested in the political economy of digital platforms, data privacy compliance, and how different regulatory regimes shape global innovation.
